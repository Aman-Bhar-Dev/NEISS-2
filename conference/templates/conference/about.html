ABOUT ASSAM UNIVERSITY

Assam University came into existence in 1994 after enactment of the Assam (Central) University Act 1989. Through its pursuit, Assam University is in the process of making itself an institute of excellence. Assam University main campus is situated at Dargakona, about 20 kms away from Silchar. The campus is set amid sprawling hillocks and typical landscape of north east. The campus is spread over 600 acres and provide an ideal environment for the researchers, students and the people interested in academic excellence. The other campus of the university is situated at Diphu in the district of Karbi Anglong, Assam. The university has the territorial jurisdiction over the five districts of Assam viz., Cachar, Sribhumi, Hailakandi, Dima Hasao (erstwhile North Cachar Hills) and Karbi Anglong. Assam University being a central university hosts a national characteristics of unity in diversity. Faculties, staff and students hail from all over the country. International students also find this institution an ideal centre for pursuing their academic endeavour.

ABOUT THE CONFERENCE

The Department of Social Work, Assam University is proud to announce the "Northeast international Sustainable Summit 2025” under the theme “Promoting Sustainable Development through Innovation, Conservation and Inclusive Growth in Northeast India". Hosted by Assam University, Silchar, Assam, India, the summit invites researchers, scholars, students, and faculty members from diverse disciplines arts, humanities, soci science, science and technology. This interdisciplinary platform aims to foster the exchange of innovative ideas, encourage collaboration, and explore how research and innovation can drive a more sustainable and inclusive future for the Northeast region. This conference aims to explore pathways for promoting sustainable development through innovation, conservation and inclusive growth in Northeast India by ensuring long-term prosperity that benefits both the environment and its people. The conference will centre on the links between preserving culture and inclusive development through traditional knowledge and how indigenous innovations form part of their modern conservation work. Empowering women and youth through entrepreneurship development along with leadership and skill development will be the focus area for equitable prosperity along with dialogue on social inequality and community marginalisation as they are relevant for social cohesion and inclusive development. The conference will also discuss how traditional handicrafts and cultural heritage can serve as a sustainable livelihood model for local communities. it will include ways into Infrastructure and Connectivity while promoting green transportation and sustainable urban planning with improvements in connectivity to unleash the full potential of Northeast India. Developing a cohesive framework for sustainable development will require working together with the government along with private sector and global partners. This conference will feature discussions with different stakeholders along with sharing of actions to help promote sustainable and inclusive development in Northeast India.

ABOUT DEPARTMENT OF SOCIAL WORK, ASSAM UNIVERSITY

The Social Work Department of Assam University, Silchar, stands as a premier center for academic excellence and social engagement, dedicated to developing skilled and socially responsible professionals. Established with the vision of addressing pressing social issues, the department offers a comprehensive range of programs, including undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral studies, that integrate rigorous theoretical knowledge with extensive practical fieldwork. Students are exposed to diverse areas such as community development, rural and urban welfare, women empowerment, health and mental well-being, social justice, and human rights, equipping them to respond effectively to the needs of society. The department places strong emphasis on research, innovation, and advocacy, encouraging students to undertake impactful projects that contribute to societal transformation and policy development. With a committed and experienced faculty, modern infrastructure, and a collaborative learning environment, the Social Work Department not only fosters academic and professional competence but also instills ethical values, empathy, and a deep sense of civic responsibility. Through active engagement with local communities, NGOs, and governmental initiatives, the department ensures that its students experience real-world challenges firsthand, preparing them to become compassionate leaders and change-makers who can drive meaningful social progress in Assam and beyond.
